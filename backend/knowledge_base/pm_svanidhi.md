# PM SVANidhi — प्रधानमंत्री स्वनिधि योजना

## Overview
PM Street Vendor's AtmaNirbhar Nidhi (PM SVANidhi) is a special micro-credit scheme for street vendors affected by COVID-19. It provides affordable working capital loans to help them resume their businesses.

## Loan Structure (Three Tiers)
- **First Loan:** Up to ₹10,000
- **Second Loan:** Up to ₹20,000 (after timely repayment of first)
- **Third Loan:** Up to ₹50,000 (after timely repayment of second)

## Key Benefits
- **7% interest subsidy** on timely/early repayment (credited directly to bank account)
- **No collateral required** — no guarantor needed
- **Digital payments incentive:** Cashback on every digital transaction (UPI, etc.)
- Tenure: 12 months per loan cycle
- Monthly repayment through easy instalments

## Eligibility
- Street vendors who have been vending on or before 24 March 2020
- Must have a Certificate of Vending (CoV) or Letter of Recommendation (LoR) from ULB
- Vendors in surrounding peri-urban/rural areas are also eligible

## How to Apply
1. Visit https://pmsvanidhi.mohua.gov.in or go to nearest bank/ULB
2. Complete self-registration with mobile number + Aadhaar
3. Get LoR from local municipal body if no CoV
4. Apply for loan — bank sanctions within 10 working days
