# PM Mudra Yojana — प्रधानमंत्री मुद्रा योजना

## Overview
Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY) provides loans up to ₹10 lakh to non-corporate, non-farm small/micro enterprises. These loans are classified as MUDRA loans under three categories:

## Categories
- **Shishu (शिशु):** Loans up to ₹50,000. For those just starting out or needing a small amount.
- **Kishore (किशोर):** Loans from ₹50,001 to ₹5,00,000. For businesses that are already set up and need expansion funds.
- **Tarun (तरुण):** Loans from ₹5,00,001 to ₹10,00,000. For well-established businesses needing more capital.

## Key Benefits
- No collateral / guarantee required
- Available at all commercial banks, RRBs, Small Finance Banks, MFIs, and NBFCs
- Interest rate varies by lender — typically 7.30% to 12% per annum
- Loan tenure: up to 5 years (may extend to 7 years for Tarun)
- Processing charges: nil or minimal

## Eligibility
- Any Indian citizen running or planning a non-farm income-generating activity
- Includes manufacturing, trading, service sector, and allied agricultural activities
- Activities like rearing of cattle, poultry, beekeeping, fisheries, etc. are also covered

## How to Apply
1. Visit the nearest bank branch or Mudra-approved NBFC
2. Fill out the MUDRA loan application form
3. Submit identity proof (Aadhaar), address proof, business plan (for Kishore/Tarun)
4. Banks cannot refuse the application without valid reason (RBI Fair Practices Code)

## Hindi Explanation (सरल भाषा में)
मुद्रा लोन एक सरकारी योजना है जो छोटे कारोबारियों को बिना गारंटी के ₹50,000 से ₹10 लाख तक का लोन देती है। अगर आप रेहड़ी, दुकान, ऑटो, सैलून, सिलाई, या कोई भी छोटा काम करते हैं तो आप इसके लिए अप्लाई कर सकते हैं। किसी भी बैंक में जाकर फॉर्म भरें — बैंक मना नहीं कर सकता बिना कारण के।
