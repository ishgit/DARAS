# Common Financial Literacy FAQs — Hindi/English

## EMI Kya Hoti Hai? / What is EMI?
EMI (Equated Monthly Instalment) wo fixed raqam hai jo aap har mahine loan chukane ke liye dete hain. Isme aapke loan ka ek hissa (principal) aur byaaj (interest) dono shamil hota hai. Jaise-jaise aap EMI bharte hain, byaaj ka hissa kam hota jaata hai aur principal ka hissa badhta jaata hai.

EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1)
Where: P = loan amount, r = monthly interest rate, n = number of months

## Reducing Balance vs Flat Rate Interest — Fark Kya Hai?
**Reducing Balance (Sahi Tarika):** Byaaj sirf bachi hui raqam par lagta hai. Jaise-jaise aap EMI bharte hain, bachi hui raqam kam hoti hai, to byaaj bhi kam hota hai.

**Flat Rate (Galat / Mehnga Tarika):** Byaaj poori loan raqam par lagta hai — pehle din se aakhiri din tak. Yeh bahut mehnga padta hai.

Example: ₹1,00,000 loan at 12% for 2 years:
- Reducing Balance: EMI ₹4,707, Total Interest ₹12,968
- Flat Rate: EMI ₹5,500, Total Interest ₹32,000 (2.5x more!)

Hamesha poochein: "Yeh reducing balance hai ya flat rate?"

## Loan-to-Income Ratio (LTI) Kya Hai?
LTI = Total Loan Amount ÷ Annual Income. Agar yeh 2 se zyada hai to aap pe zyada karaz hai. Banks generally 3x se zyada loan nahi dete.

## FOIR (Fixed Obligation to Income Ratio) Kya Hai?
FOIR = (Sabhi EMI + fixed kharche) ÷ Monthly Income × 100
- 30% se kam: Bahut safe
- 30-50%: Thoda risky
- 50% se zyada: Khatarnaak — aur loan mat lein

## Debt Trap (Karz Ka Jaal) Se Kaise Bachein?
1. Kabhi byaaj chukane ke liye naya loan mat lein ("loan pe loan")
2. Sahukaar se bacchein — bank ya SHG se lein
3. EMI aapki bachat ka 40% se zyada na ho
4. Emergency fund rakhein — kam se kam 3 mahine ka kharcha
5. Loan lene se pehle poochein: "Agar meri income band ho jaaye to kya hoga?"

## Agar Loan Nahi Chuka Pa Rahe To?
1. Bank se baat karein — moratorium ya restructuring maangein
2. RBI Loan Restructuring scheme ka fayda uthayein
3. Legal help lein — NALSA se free vakeel milte hain
4. Sahukaar ki dhamki pe 100 dial karein — yeh illegal hai
5. NGO se madad lein (M3M Foundation, Rang De, Ujjivan)

## Credit Score Kya Hai?
CIBIL Score 300-900 ke beech hota hai. 750+ achha maana jaata hai.
- Samay pe EMI bharein → score badhta hai
- Loan default karein → score girta hai aur future mein loan nahi milega
- CIBIL report free mein dekh sakte hain: mycibil.com

## Insurance Zaroori Kyun Hai?
Agar aap akele kamaane waale hain aur loan liya hai, to life insurance zaroori hai.
- PMJJBY: ₹436/saal = ₹2 lakh cover
- PMSBY: ₹20/saal = ₹2 lakh accident cover
- Dono Jan Dhan account se le sakte hain
